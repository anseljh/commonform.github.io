Common Form

composable, verifiable, shareable legal contracts

Common Form lets attorneys, companies, and computer systems share forms, provisions, definitions, and other contract components free of client confidential information. It’s also the foundation for a set of tools that manage contract structure, leaving you free to focus on substance.
